Rules of the Game
How do I play Scorchy Slots? Scorchy Slots costs 5 NP to play. In order to start a new game, click on the Play Again button. This will automatically deduct 5 NP from you.
What is the aim of the game? You need to get three or four of the same item in a row on the "WIN" line in order to win. If you have three of the same in the "WIN" line, but there is another item between them, you have not won.
What can I win? You can win anything from the small prize of 1 NP up to the grand prize of the jackpot. You can even win all 6 faeries (one of each type). The available prizes are shown in detail here.
What are holds? To help you get a win you may be given the chance to hold. If so, you will see four check boxes under each reel. If you check a box and then spin the reels, the reel you have checked will hold position and not spin.
What does the feature do? You may notice that some of the fruits have numbers on them. If you get a fruit with a number three on it, then the feature bar lights up three places and so on. Sometimes your progress up the feature bar is held until the next go and any additional numbers you get are added on. If you light up all the numbers on the feature bar you win a random prize. This could be a very good prize, or a not-so-great thing. You may even lose NP!
